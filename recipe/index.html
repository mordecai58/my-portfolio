HOW TO MAKE CHAPATI

Ingredients

  • 140g whole meal flour
  • 140g plain flour,plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil,plus extra for greasing
  • 180ml hot or as needed

METHOD

  • STEP 1: In a large bowl,stir together the flours and salt.Use a wooden-spoon to stir in the olive oil and enough water to make a soft dough that is elastic but not sticky.
  • STEP 2: Knead the dough on lightly floured surface for 5-10 mins until it is smooth.Divide 10 pieces,or less if you want bigger breads.Roll each piece into a ball.Let rest for a few mins.
  • STEP 3: Place the rolled dough on medium fire when brown patches appear flip
  • STEP 4: When ready place on a separate plate with a small towel to remove the oil
  • STEP 5: Serve with some gravy or stew of choice
